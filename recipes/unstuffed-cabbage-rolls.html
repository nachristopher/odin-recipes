Unstuffed Cabbage Rolls

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cabbage
  • 1 pound ground sausage or ground beef
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons pepper
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 cups vegetable broth

Steps

  1. Dice your onion and chop the cabbage into chunks
  2. In a medium skillet, brown your choice of ground meat and add in the diced onions
  3. Drain grease from the meat and add meat to a large pot
  4. Stir in the cabbage until thorougly combined with the meat
  5. Stir in both cans of diced tomatoes
  6. Add in vegetable broth
  7. Cook for 30 minutes or until cabbage is tender
  8. Add salt and pepper, serve into bowls and enjoy!
