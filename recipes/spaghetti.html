Spaghetti

Description

This will be a simple recipe for quick and delicious Spaghetti!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 box of spaghetti noodles
  • 4 quarts of water
  • 2 jars of your favorite pasta sauce

Steps

For the Sauce

  1. Heat a medium pot over medium heat.
  2. Once pot is heated, add 1 pound ground beef.
  3. Brown meat, stirring constantly until it is fully cooked through.
  4. Remove from heat and drain grease from the meat.
  5. Once the grease has been drained, mix in your jars of pastra sauce.
  6. Voila! Your spaghetti sauce is complete! Serve over top your cooked noodles or mix it in. it is your choice!

For the noodles

  1. In a large pot, bring your 4 quarts of water to a boil over high heat
  2. Once water is boiling, add in 1 box spaghetti pasta and return to a boil
  3. Once pasta has returned to a boil, continue to cook for 4-5 miuutes stirring constantly or until noodles are tender
  4. Once pasta is tender, remove from heat and drain water from pasta
  5. Once water has been drained, add your sauce and enjoy your meal!
